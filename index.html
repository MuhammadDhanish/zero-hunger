About Us

We believe that good food belongs to people, not landfills.

Every day, tons of perfectly good food from events, restaurants, and grocery stores goes to waste. At the same time, millions struggle with food insecurity. We exist to fix this broken system by connecting local food surpluses directly with communities and shelters in need.

  • Zero Waste: We rescue edible surplus before it is thrown away.
  • Direct Impact: Food is delivered directly to vetted shelters and NGOs.
  • Community Driven: Powered by hundreds of local volunteers.
How It Works

Three easy steps to transform waste into hope.

Our streamlined platform makes it simple for anyone to contribute to a hunger-free world.

1

Connect

Local restaurants, event organizers, and individuals list their surplus food through our simple platform in minutes.

2

Collect

Our trained volunteer network coordinates swift, hygienic pickups, ensuring food safety standards are met every time.

3

Distribute

Nutritious meals are delivered directly to shelters, food banks, and communities in need across 30+ cities.
