About Us

We believe good food belongs to people, not landfills.

Every day, tons of perfectly good food from events, restaurants, and grocery stores goes to waste. At the same time, millions struggle with food insecurity. We exist to fix this broken system.

  • ✓
    Zero Waste We rescue edible surplus before it is thrown away, ensuring nothing goes to landfill.
  • ✓
    Direct Impact Food is delivered directly to vetted shelters, NGOs, and communities that need it most.
  • ✓
    Community Driven Powered by hundreds of passionate local volunteers who give their time every day.
How It Works

Three steps to transform waste into hope.

Our streamlined platform makes it simple for anyone to contribute to a hunger-free world in minutes.

Step 01

Connect

Restaurants, event organizers, and individuals list their surplus food through our platform in just minutes.

Step 02

Collect

Our trained volunteer network coordinates swift, hygienic pickups — ensuring food safety standards every time.

Step 03

Distribute

Nutritious meals are delivered directly to shelters, food banks, and communities in need across 30+ cities.
