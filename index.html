Who We Are

A Community Driven by Purpose

Zero Hunger Connection is a non-profit technology platform connecting food donors with those who need it most. We believe no meal should go to waste while people go hungry. Through our smart logistics network and passionate volunteer community, we turn surplus into sustenance.

  • Zero administrative friction for donors
  • Real-time tracking from pickup to delivery
  • Vetted NGO & shelter partners across 30+ cities
